Policy

When you buy a ticket, all customers will be subject to festival policies, otherwise tickets and enteries maybe declared invalid.

These policies are:
Attendies must be of 18 years old or over to attend.
Any of these items are strictly prohibated: BBQs, Firepits, Gas canisters, eggs, flares, fireworks.
Any weapons are also strictly proibited.
SonicBoom Festival has a strict no drugs Policy. If you are found to be in possession of any drugs you will be expelled from the festival and handed over to the local police.
