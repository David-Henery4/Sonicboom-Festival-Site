Experience

Stages

There are many different stages at Sonic Boom, thirteen to be exact! These stages will play host to wide range of electronic music from House, Alternative, Garage, Jungle, Drum & Bass, Trance, Synthwave and many more genres & sub genres for you to experience!

Activites

We also have many more activites for you to explore such as silent discos, rave tents, D.J workshops and rides.

Food & Drink

Experience a diverse range of food and drink coming from all over the world! From South American foods to German beer tents theres always something for everybody.

Timeout

Looking for somewhere to take a break and have a little rest and chill? We have just the places for you! We have different Lounge Bars across the site for you, with DJs playing chill music and waiters on hand for your every need.

We Hope to see you next year!
